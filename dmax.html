Introducing The Isuzu D-Max

The Isuzu D-Max Yukon has the same spec as the Eiger but with 17" alloys, the luxury of a leather steering wheel (with audio and cruise controls), chrome grille and rear bumper and in order to keep its beauty pristine, a load liner. - See more at: http://isuzu.co.uk/the-range/yukon/yukon-overview/#sthash.BsOphB51.dpuf

The Yukon doesn't just look amazing: with a six-speaker audio system which includes 2 Exciter speakers embedded in the roof lining, it sounds amazing too.
